My Term Project

In the recent semester, I developed "Art Gallery," an online art gallery platform tailored to bridge the gap between emerging artists and art aficionados. The platform empowers artists with tools to showcase their work, while art enthusiasts enjoy a curated experience to discover and purchase unique pieces. Prioritizing security, I integrated phone number authentication for genuine user registrations. The dynamic profile system, coupled with an intuitive navigation interface, ensures both artists and buyers have a personalized, engaging experience. This project symbolizes my commitment to enriching the art community by leveraging digital solutions.
